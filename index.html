Who We Are

Absoc Mental Health is a youth-led initiative dedicated to empowering the Muslim community through culturally-sensitive mental health resources.

What We Offer

Workshops

Interactive sessions on mental well-being, mindfulness, and stress relief.

Peer Support

A safe space to talk and receive support from trained community members.

Resources

Access Islamic and general mental health guides, articles, and videos.
